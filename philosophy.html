Dharma

Dharma refers to righteous conduct, moral duty, and the ethical way of living that sustains harmony in society.

Karma

Karma is the principle of cause and effect, stating that every action has consequences that shape one’s future.

Moksha

Moksha represents liberation from the cycle of birth and death and the realization of ultimate truth.

Bhakti and Jnana

Bhakti emphasizes devotion and surrender, while Jnana focuses on knowledge and wisdom as paths to spiritual growth.

Shruti Setu

Shruti Setu explores the rich heritage of Indian scriptures and philosophy, connecting ancient wisdom with contemporary thought through a clean and academic digital platform.
