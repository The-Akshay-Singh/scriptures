Welcome to Shruti Setu

Shruti Setu is a digital bridge connecting the timeless wisdom of Indian scriptures with the modern world. Indian sacred texts are not merely religious writings; they are profound sources of philosophy, ethics, science, and spiritual inquiry that have guided human thought for thousands of years.

This website aims to introduce readers to the richness of India’s scriptural heritage and highlight its relevance in contemporary life.

📜 What Does “Shruti Setu” Mean?

The word Shruti refers to knowledge that is heard and revealed, primarily associated with the earliest Indian scriptures. Setu means a bridge.

Together, Shruti Setu symbolizes a bridge between ancient wisdom and present-day understanding.
