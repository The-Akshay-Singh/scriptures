Shruti Setu is an academic initiative created to promote awareness of Indian scriptural heritage.

The website presents ancient knowledge in a structured and accessible digital format, helping students understand its historical, philosophical, and cultural significance.

This project demonstrates how technology can be used to preserve and share traditional wisdom responsibly.

Shruti Setu

Shruti Setu explores the rich heritage of Indian scriptures and philosophy, connecting ancient wisdom with contemporary thought through a clean and academic digital platform.
